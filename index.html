How Smart Society Works

Getting started with Smart Society is simple. Follow these easy steps:

Step 1: Download the App

Start by downloading the Smart Society app from the App Store or Google Play to get started with the community-focused features.

Step 2: Login with Your Mobile Number

Once installed, sign up using your mobile number to authenticate and create your account. It’s quick and easy! You'll receive an OTP for verification.

Step 3: Enjoy the Features

Now that you're logged in, explore the app’s features: post announcements, collaborate with neighbors, access local services, and more!
